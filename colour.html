The color is #______

Hexadecimal colours are commonly used in websites and software applications, specified with the format:
#RRGGBB - RR (red), GG (green) and BB (blue)
Each hexadecimal integer ranges between 00 and FF, which specifies the intensity of the color.
For example, #FFFFFF represents the colour 'white' as all RGB colours are at full intensity,
while #000000 represents 'black' as all are at it's lowest intensity.
